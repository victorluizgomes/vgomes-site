Lucid Paths is an exclusive collection of 1,000 generative NFTs — each one a unique pairing of algorithmic visual art and procedurally-generated music. The project was one of Lua Labs' earliest Web3 ventures, built to explore what fully generative, audiovisual blockchain art could look like.

## Highlights

- **200+ ETH** in secondary trading volume
- **Trended above Fidenzas** on OpenSea for 24 hours at launch — a benchmark moment in the generative art space
- Every NFT pairs a unique visual composition with its own algorithmically composed soundtrack

## How It Was Built

The art and audio were both generated using **p5.js**, with the sound package handling algorithmic music composition. The algorithm was refined iteratively over several weeks to ensure every piece felt distinct and high-quality — then manually curated before the reveal.

Smart contracts were written in **Solidity** as ERC-721 on Ethereum, with all assets and metadata hosted on **IPFS via Pinata**. Shell scripts handled the batch processing pipeline for audio/video rendering and metadata packaging.
