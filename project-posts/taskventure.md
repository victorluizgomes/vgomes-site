Taskventure turns productivity into an RPG. Every task you complete is a quest. Every habit you maintain builds a streak. And as you make progress in real life, your pixel art character evolves — leveling up, equipping new gear, and growing alongside you.

![Taskventure landing page](/projects/taskventure/taskventure-hero.png "Turn Your To-Dos and Habits into an RPG Game")

![Taskventure app example screenshot](/projects/taskventure/taskventure-app.png "Taskventure app home page screenshot")

## Core Features

- **Character creation & progression** — craft your hero in a pixel art fantasy world; they grow as you do
- **Equipment & inventory** — earn gear by leveling up, with attribute boosts and cosmetic customization
- **Quests (tasks)** — set due dates, assign priorities, and track completion
- **Habit streaks** — build daily habits with streak tracking woven into the game loop
- **Free to start** — no paywall to get going

## Behind the Scenes

The app was built with **Next.js**, **React**, and **TypeScript** on the frontend, with a pixel art aesthetic designed to feel like a classic JRPG. A v2 was explored as a **React Native** mobile app to bring the experience natively to iOS and Android — but the complexity of rebuilding the game systems for native mobile proved too steep, and the web app remained the focus.
